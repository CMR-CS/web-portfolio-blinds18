    • Glacier National Park

    Glacier National Park is one of the most popular National Parks in the United States,
    and also has some of the most beautiful hiking trails in the state.

    Hiking Trails

    1. Iceberg Lake
      • Hike to a cobalt-blue lake below jaw-dropping cliffs with namesake chunks of ice
    2. Hidden Lake
      • Start at the famous Logan Pass, work your way across flower-covered alpine meadows, then along a glacial moraine to an overlook of the gorgeous Hidden lake tucked among jagged peaks
    3. Avalanche Lake
      • Walk among a cool cedar and hemlock forest along Avalanche Creek as it flows through a tight gorge, then continue through the forest towards the beautiful Avalanche Lake surrounded by steep cliffs and fantastic waterfalls
