    • Hiking Trails

    1. MT.Washburn
      • Hike up an old road past open meadows and gnarled rock faces to the panoramic views of Yellowstone at the lookout.
    2. North Rim Trail
      • Follow along the rim of the canyon of the mighty Yellowstone River as it changes from a slow, broad river to a frothing whitewater rapid that spills over into two incredible waterfalls.
    3. Avalanche Peak
      • This steep trail in the Absoroka Range winds through a beautiful forest, until you are able to take in the unobstructed views of the mountains and Yellowstone Lake from the peak's shoulder to summit.
